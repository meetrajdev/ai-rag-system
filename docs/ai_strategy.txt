AI Strategy Document

We are integrating Generative AI into our systems to improve productivity and automation.

AI systems should use Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to ensure accurate and up-to-date responses.

Vector databases are used to store embeddings of documents for semantic search.

We use OpenAI APIs and other LLM providers for AI capabilities.

All AI systems must include monitoring, logging, and guardrails to ensure safe usage.
